Self-Care Strategies

While professional help is important, these daily practices can support your mental wellbeing:

Run

Move Your Body

Even 10 minutes of walking can boost your mood. Exercise releases endorphins that improve mental health.

Sleep

Prioritize Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours. Good sleep hygiene includes a consistent schedule and limiting screens before bed.

Handshake

Stay Connected

Reach out to friends, family, or support groups. Social connection is vital for mental health.

Meditate

Practice Mindfulness

Try meditation, deep breathing, or grounding exercises to manage stress and anxiety.

Salad

Nourish Yourself

Eat regular, balanced meals. What you eat affects how you feel, both physically and mentally.

Write

Express Yourself

Journal, create art, or talk to someone. Processing emotions helps prevent them from building up.

No

Limit Substances

Reduce alcohol and avoid drugs. They may provide temporary relief but worsen mental health over time.

Target

Set Small Goals

Break tasks into manageable steps. Small accomplishments build momentum and confidence.
