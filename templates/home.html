What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

1 in 5 Adults

Experience mental illness each year in the United States. Many more experience stress or other concerns.

Treatment Works

Between 70-90% of people who seek treatment see significant improvement.

Early Help Matters

Getting support early can prevent conditions from worsening.
