Mental Health Includes Our Emotional, Psychological, and Social Well-Being

Mental health is important at every stage of life, especially in Kenya where 1 in 4 adults experiences it annually (WHO, 2025)

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.

In Kenya, mental health challenges affect millions, but support is available through community and national resources.
